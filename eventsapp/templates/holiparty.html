Special Holi Trip to Goa

Holi is the festival of color. Its is widely celebrated festival in the month of March in India. Holi is celebrated across the nation and each destination have an epic style or version of celebration. Holi celebration range from old-age tradition, unique custom to modern parties with music & dance, bhang and plenty of colors.

Holi, festival of color is celebrated in Goa with great joy. People who travel to Goa during Holi can feast their eyes on the rain of colors. Color of festival is enjoyed by the local and tourist alike. People from all communities Hindus, Christians and local & foreigner are invited to Holi parties that are being organised outdoor premises or even at the beach side resorts to play Holi with music & dance and plenty of color.

AllRoadsLeadToGoa, is organizing special trip to Goa during Holi with wild celebration & party in Goan style. Let's celebrate holi away from home this year and experience festival of color in different style of fun. We make sure that you cherish every moment of the trip spent at party capital of India, Goa.
